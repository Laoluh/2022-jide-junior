Our Mission to provide clean water

Sep. 15th 21

On 22nd of March 2021, UNICEF in her press release noted that "nearly one third of Nigerian children do not have access to enough water to meet their daily need. and that "one in five children globally do not have aceess to enough water to meet their everyday needs." It is then obviuos that although efforts of government towards improving access to water may be on the rise, it is still not enough to bridge the about 26% deficit in the margin of children with lack of access to water, a figure that was described as alarming. With the aim of contributing to the progress towards acheiving the SDGs on water, we have mapped out areas where we can achieve immediate progress in the bridge of the inequality of access gap and are determined to provide clean water to 20 selected communities.
