We are intentional about improving the health care system, we want to provide accessible primary health care.

we are on the journey to make primary health care accessible

Sep. 15th 21

We are Commited to delivering health service to as many as possible, it is not news that the health care infrastructure in nigeria can not take care of the many health needs of the Nigerian populace, overtime it is seeming that health care is getting more expensive. as part of our vision, we are commited to reducing the inequality gap. We are on a mission to identify person who lack access to quality health care and assist them through our outreaches and sponsorship where applicable. we welcome you to join us on the journey to impact the health care system. Together we can achieve so much more than we imagine.

Improving Cancer Registry

Sep. 15th 21

As part of our mission to improve the health care system we are commited to improving cancer registry in rural nigeria, as we have discovered that there is a wide margin that needs to be covered in the area of cancer registration. other than the expensive and unaccessible nature of diagnosis and treatment, enough awareness is still a major factor that has affected the registration of cancer. we are committed to spreading the word and aiding the access, as early detection can be a life saver. you are welcome to volunteer, donate and reach out to us on behalf of someone.
